About Me

Hi, I'm Jean!

I live in New York and I graduated from Stony Brook University with a major in Technological Systems Management and a minor in Digital Arts.

I have always loved art and anything aesthetically pleasing. So I figured if I wanted to pursue something in higher education, I would find a way to apply my love for art to something everyone would need, technology!

Along the way, I picked up many mediums and forms of art. I started from traditonal, hand-drawn art to digital art and photography. Because of my background in technology, I became familiar with coding and was I was able to pick up web design and learned how to make websites both functional and pleasing to the eye for the user's experience.
